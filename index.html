About Kofer University

Located in the heart of Monsey, NY, Kofer University is a thriving institution with over 4,500 students. We are committed to academic excellence, innovative research, and creating a supportive community for our students to succeed.

Academics

Our diverse range of academic programs is designed to prepare students for the challenges of the modern world. From undergraduate degrees to specialized graduate programs, Kofer University offers something for everyone.
